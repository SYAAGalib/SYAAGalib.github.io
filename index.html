About Me

Hi, I'm SYAAGalib, a passionate software developer specializing in Python, C/C++, Rust, Laravel, Django, React Native, and more. I am currently completing my CSE studies at Northern University of Business and Technology in Khulna. I also work as a freelancer, specializing in websites, mobile apps, PC applications, and AI/ML-based solutions.

I'm currently exploring and developing bespoke Large Language Models (LLMs) to uncover new AI possibilities. Additionally, I co-founded AIELTS, a startup that was recognized and awarded by the Bangladesh ICT Ministry and Hi-Tech Park Bangladesh for its innovative contributions.

Whether it's creating cutting-edge AI solutions or building robust applications, I'm enthusiastic about pushing the frontiers of technology to solve real-world challenges.
